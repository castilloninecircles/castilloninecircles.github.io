About The Game

CASTILLO: The Nine Circles is a retro FPS Collectathon mixing the exploration from 90's platformers with all the carnage you love from classic shooters.

You play as Dracula. Your throne has been stolen by a time-traveling usurper who has ripped apart reality to keep you trapped in the past. Armed with only a gun and your wits, you must traverse the nine circles of Hell, reclaim your power, and take back what is yours.

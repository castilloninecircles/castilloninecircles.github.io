Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

Your privacy matters. CASTILLO: The Nine Circles is a single-player PC game and does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information. The only data the game stores is save data kept locally on your computer so you can keep your progress.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information or analytics.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising identifiers
  • Gameplay analytics/telemetry
  • Crash reports sent to us
  • Any data from your contacts, photos, microphone, or camera

Local Save Data

The game stores save progress locally on your PC. This may include:

  • Game progress (e.g., checkpoints, completed levels, unlocks)
  • Settings and preferences (e.g., audio, graphics, controls)

This save data:

  • Stays on your device
  • Is not transmitted to us
  • Can typically be removed by uninstalling the game and/or deleting the game’s local save files (exact location varies by system and configuration)

Online Features

CASTILLO: The Nine Circles does not require an internet connection to play and does not include online features that transmit player data.

If this changes in a future update, this policy will be updated before any data collection occurs.

Third-Party Services

CASTILLO: The Nine Circles does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

If you download or play the game through a third-party platform (such as a game store or launcher), that platform may collect data according to its own privacy policy. We do not control or receive that data unless explicitly stated elsewhere.

Children’s Privacy

The game does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. Since no personal data is collected, no child data is collected.

Data Security

Because we do not collect or store data on our servers, we do not maintain player databases. Local save data is stored on your device and is protected by your device’s own security controls.

Changes to This Policy

If we change how the game handles data, we will update this policy and revise the Effective Date above.
